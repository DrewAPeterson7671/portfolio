About

How I Got Started

My enthusiasm to code started in high school. I remember pulling up to a stop light in my car and thinking "If weight sensor > 500lbs, then...". Learning to code had changed me. I was always abstracting the systems I saw in world around me in my mind.

Life took me in different direction from coding. In other careers, I was always tinkering with spreadsheets. Analyzing sales trends and trying to uncover where to focus my efforts for greatest efficiency. Automating complex forms into a form that output the paperwork in PDF form. I was always trying to automate my tasks.

In 2018, I had a chance meeting with a manager of a coding bootcamp. I had heard of them vaguely, but was unsure about their viability. 45 minutes later, I had a mission. I could have this. I could change course, but many sacrifices would have to be made. I began to prepare and gather resources to make it so.

The journey continues...
